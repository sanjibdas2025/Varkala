A Soulful Escape to Varkala

Cliffside Cafés, Canoe Rides & Coastal Calm | July 25–29

Intro Itinerary Travel Tips

Why Varkala?

After months of city hustle, I craved a quiet coastal break with good coffee, soft sands, and a touch of soul. Varkala—with its red cliffs, yoga vibes, and offbeat charm—felt like the perfect monsoon getaway.

Day-by-Day Itinerary

Day 1: Late Arrival

Arrived at Thiruvananthapuram Airport around 9:20 PM. Reached Molly’s Hostel, which had the warmest host and chill vibe.

Day 2: Cliffs, Cafés & Ocean Sounds

  • Breakfast at God’s Own Café
  • Sunset at Black Sand Beach
  • Chill evening at Darjeeling Café

Day 3: Jatayu Earth’s Center

Visited the world's largest bird sculpture with stunning views after a short cable car ride.

Day 4: Munroe Island

Took a sunrise canoe ride through peaceful backwaters—serene and unforgettable.

Day 5: Thiruvananthapuram

Explored Padmanabhaswamy Temple and Napier Museum before heading to the airport.

Quick Travel Tips

  • Best Time: Oct–March (monsoon = moody magic)
  • Getting Around: Scooters or local trains
  • Budget: ₹7,000–₹12,000 for 5 days
  • Stay: Molly’s Hostel (highly recommended!)
